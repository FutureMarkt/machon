About us
עלינו

learn more

The role of a community Rabbi holds a powerful potential: he can and should be so much more than an answerer of halachic questions.  A Rabbi has the dynamic capacity to be a leader who guides people spiritually, far beyond shiurim and divrei Torah.

In the fall of 2023, Kehilat Shirat David will launch a unique institute that will play a critical role in the Efrat community and throughout Eretz Yisrael for years to come.

(Our goals)

  1. training Future Leaders for the Growing Anglo Community in Israel
    2. בסייד
  2. strengthening the Zayit and the Broader Efrat Community

Ben De Toledo

Ben grew up in Los Angeles, CA. Ben studied at the Shappell's Darche Noam Yeshiva and the Pardes Institute of Jewish Studies. He holds a bachelor's degree in Sociology from Columbia University and a second bachelor's degree in Jewish History from The Jewish Theological Seminary of America. Ben is a member of Ohr Torah Stones' Straus Rabbinical Seminary at Yeshivat Machanaim in Migdal Oz. Ben will be joining us after finishing his term as a Rabbi in residence at the Abrahamic Family Houses' Moses Ben Maimon Synagogue in Abu Dhabi. Ben and his wife Yael, are excited to join the Shirat David community!
