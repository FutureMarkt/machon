Rabbi Dr. Rosh Beit Midrash

Yosef Bronstein

Rabbi Dr. Yosef Bronstein will lead MZH’s morning seder. A student of Rav Aharon Lichtenstein and Rav Michoel Rosensweig, Rav Yosef is trained in the world of traditional in-depth study of Gemara and Halacha. After receiving semicha from Yeshiva University’s RIETS, Rav Yosef received a PhD in Talmudic Studies from Yeshiva University with a focus on tannaitic midrash. For the past decade, Rav Yosef has taught Jewish thought and Halacha at Yeshiva University and other post-high school institutions such as Michlelet Mevaseret Yerushalayim (MMY) and has developed warm relationships with a wide array of students.

He is the coauthor of Reshimos Shiurim al Masechet Kiddushin (Rav Soloveitchik’s Gemara shiurim on masechet Kiddushin) and the author of Engaging the Essence: The Philosophy of the Lubavitcher Rebbe (forthcoming from Maggid Books).

Rabbi Rav of Kehilat Shirat David & Rosh Machon

Shlomo Katz

Rav Shlomo’s leadership blends his deep knowledge of Torah with a thirst for a lived experience of closeness to Hashem. His spiritual authenticity is infectious and the passion he exhibits for Torah, the Jewish people and Eretz Yisrael reverberates throughout his kehillah.

With hundreds of students, and thousands of recorded shiurim, published books and many moving recorded albums, he is uniquely suited to guide young rabbis to lives of communal impact in Eretz Yisrael. Rav Shlomo will oversee all aspects of MZH and will be directly leading the afternoon study and workshops in Penimiyus Hatorah and rabbinical training.
